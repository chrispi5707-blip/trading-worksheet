4 PAUSE RULES
What is a Pause?

Brief consolidation within a momentum move (on 2-min chart) where price temporarily stops pushing before continuation.

‚úÖ Ideal Pause (Score 5)
  • 2-3 candles (4-6 minutes)
  • Volume DROPPING on pause
  • Small-bodied candles
  • Price respects 10 EMA
  • No closes wrong side of 10 EMA
Entry Mechanics

LONG: Price breaks ABOVE highest point of pause candles

SHORT: Price breaks BELOW lowest point of pause candles

Stop Placement:

LONG: Below lowest point of pause | SHORT: Above highest point of pause

‚ùå Skip These Pauses
  • 6+ candles (momentum died)
  • Volume increasing during pause
  • Multiple closes wrong side of 10 EMA
